Your work is amazing. But nobody knows it.

Engineers build systems but often forget to document their own impact.
PromoteMe aggregates your contributions from GitHub and task management systems into data-driven brag documents.

Install CLI
$ cargo install promoteme
user@localhost ~

Features

Automated Discovery

Eliminate manual searching. Connect your engineering toolstack to automatically retrieve and categorize your work history.

Objective Impact

Transform raw commit logs into business-readable summaries. Quantify your contributions with data, not just memory.

Documentation Enforcement

Forgetting to document work is a common point of failure. PromoteMe ensures your achievements are permanently recorded.

Your document, your data

Use your own tools to generate the report. You just need the GitHub CLI and the magic happens.

AI-Powered Synthesis

Transform raw commit logs into compelling narratives. AI analyzes your PRs and generates business-readable impact summaries.

Multi-language Support

Generate reports in any language. Perfect for international teams and global organizations.
